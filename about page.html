About us        







Our factory is located at Mid baneshwor near syanko. We are making hennas since
2011 B.S. We make henna from a powder, start by mixing your powder with lemon
juice or apple cider vinegar, sugar, and an essential oil like lavender or tea tree in
machine.Once we have a henna paste, we store it in a dry, airtight container for at least
1 day before sending it to marketit since it take this long to release the most vibrant colors.











We offer you not only henna products like
stickers and cones but also we offer you to
put it on anywhere any anytime within or
outside Nepal. We do many styles like Indo-arabic,heavy bridal,
simple and aesthetic hennas.
